Conveyor Belt System: Laying the Foundation for Smarter Waste Sorting

As part of our ongoing mission to build intelligent, tech-driven waste solutions, weve developed a 3-meter-long conveyor belt system a critical component in the future of automated waste sorting.

Driven by a NEMA 34 stepper motor, this custom-built conveyor is designed to carry waste materials for sensor-based analysis and sorting. In future iterations, it will serve as the input channel for our smart sorting units, enabling continuous, hands-free waste processing.

Built for Integration

This conveyor is more than just a moving belt its the backbone of a larger system in the making. It ensures:

  • Steady, controlled movement of waste items

  • Precise positioning for material detection sensors

  • Scalability, designed to be integrated with smart bins, robotic arms, or vision systems

Whether its plastic, metal, or glass, every item begins its journey on this belt the first step in transforming how we handle waste.

This build marks a key milestone in our roadmap toward end-to-end automated waste management. Its built strong, runs smooth, and is ready for what comes next.

The future of waste sorting is moving  literally.
